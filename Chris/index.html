Weed MD – Your Guide to Medicinal Marijuana

WeedMD recommends a marijuana strain based on the your ailments. We aim to educate and empower medical marijuana users to target their own symptoms and manage them more effectively

This initial recommendation service is built upon current research. As we gain user's WeedMD will be able to take their experiences with a strain into account with a rating system. Ultimately, the system will become so good at making recommendations it will begin to create its own modified strains of marijuana; one prescription will be able to cover a multitude of ailments.
